Frequently asked questions

How do I earn points?

Complete tasks posted by other creators — following, liking, watching, or joining — and you'll earn points automatically once the task is verified.

Why didn't I get points for a task I completed?

Points are only awarded after our automated checks confirm genuine activity. If a task looked suspicious (for example, too little time spent on the page) it may not have counted. Contact support if you think this was a mistake.

How long do point purchases take to process?

Purchases are reviewed manually and are typically approved within 24 hours of submitting your payment reference.

How do I report a fake or broken task?

Use the report button on the task itself, or contact support directly with the task link and details.

My account was suspended — what do I do?

Email support@nexorealm.org or reach out on Discord and we'll review your case.
