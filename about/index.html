About Next Boost

A free engagement exchange built for creators who are tired of choosing between paying for fake followers or growing alone.

What we're building

Next Boost is a platform where creators trade real engagement with each other. Instead of buying followers, views, or members from bot farms, you complete small tasks for other creators — following, liking, watching, joining — and earn points you spend to get the same kind of support back on your own content and channels.

Every action on Next Boost comes from a real person using their own account. No bots, no fake engagement, no purchased follower counts that disappear a month later.

Why we started this

Growing an audience from zero is genuinely hard, and the fastest "solutions" — buying followers, views, or members — actively hurt creators in the long run. Platforms detect and purge fake engagement, algorithms deprioritize accounts with suspicious activity, and an inflated follower count with no real interaction is easy for anyone to spot. We built Next Boost as a straightforward alternative: real engagement, earned by giving real engagement first.

How it works, briefly

  1. Sign up and get points to start.
  2. Complete tasks — follow, like, watch, or join — for other creators to earn points.
  3. Spend those points posting your own tasks to grow your following, your channel, or your server.

We also run anti-cheat checks on every task completion, so points are only awarded for genuine engagement, and we take reports of fake or spammy tasks seriously.

Questions about how it works?

Check out our support page, or reach out directly — we're happy to help.
